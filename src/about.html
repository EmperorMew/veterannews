Our Mission

Ground truth for those who served.

Veteran News is the daily intelligence briefing for U.S. veterans, service members, and the families who stand with them. We believe veterans deserve the same level of editorial care that the Pentagon gets — every morning, on every story.

Who we are

Veteran News is published by Warriors Fund, an independent nonprofit dedicated to supporting warriors and their families through advocacy, financial assistance, and community programs. Every story we run is part of that larger mission: equip veterans with the information they need to claim what they've earned, navigate what's been promised, and connect with the brothers and sisters who get it.

Why we exist

Veterans are the most-talked-about, least-listened-to constituency in America. Press releases announce new programs that veterans never hear about. Benefits change without explanation. Crises pass before the people closest to them know. We built Veteran News to close that gap — not to chase clicks, not to sell ads, not to push an agenda. Just the facts veterans need, surfaced in the time they have.

How we curate

We aggregate from 14 vetted sources — including the VA News, Military Times, Task & Purpose, The War Horse, DAV, We Are The Mighty, and the official military branch news outlets. Sources are tiered by trust and editorial rigor. Every six hours our system pulls the latest, classifies stories by category (benefits, health, transition, advocacy, legacy, community, family, service), de-duplicates, and surfaces the most important to the front of the briefing.

We don't write opinion. We don't editorialize. We don't manufacture outrage. We pick the strongest reporting from the strongest journalists covering the veteran beat — and put it in one place, organized for the way veterans read.

Our editorial standards

  • Sources are named. Every story carries the publication that reported it.
  • Originals are linked. If you want the full story, one click takes you to the source.
  • No paywalls between veterans and information they need. Free, always.
  • No ads. Our independence is funded by Warriors Fund and reader donations.
  • Crisis support stays one tap away. Always 988 in the masthead, always.

About Warriors Fund

Warriors Fund is the parent organization behind Veteran News. The fund supports warriors and their families through direct financial assistance, mental health and wellness programs, family support services, and policy advocacy in Washington and state capitals.

Veteran News is one of the ways the fund extends its reach — getting accurate, timely information into veterans' hands at scale, while keeping the door open for those who need direct help to find it.
