Where your gift goes

Warriors Fund operates programs across four areas — and every dollar is tracked and reported in our annual financials.

  • Direct financial assistance. Emergency grants for veterans and families in financial crisis — rent, utilities, vehicle repair, medical bills.
  • Wellness programs. Mental health, peer support, and recovery initiatives for veterans dealing with the invisible costs of service.
  • Family support. Caregiver assistance, survivor support, and programs that strengthen the home front.
  • Information and advocacy. Veteran News, policy advocacy, and the work of getting accurate information to those who need it.
